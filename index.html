Chapter II · About Us

Rooted in culture, crafted for cinema

TFC Films & CO. began with one belief: every celebration carries a story worth telling on the big screen. We are passionate creatives who love narrating, capturing, and epitomizing tales of love, laughter, and togetherness across cultures.

Over the years we have covered hundreds of multi-cultural weddings across the globe — each film a means to seal a moment you can savour for a lifetime.

Chapter II · Our Style

Authentic mood, artistic impact

Our style is capturing moments in their true authentic mood, with a touch of artistry for an everlasting impact. We chase natural expressions, cinematic angles, and portraits with a touch of regality.

It is not just about the wow moments — but how they are captured: lighting, composition, headroom, and angles that cut the noise and make every frame enchanting.

Chapter II · Why TFC
